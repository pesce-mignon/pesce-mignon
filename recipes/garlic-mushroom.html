Garlic Mushroom

Source — Feb 17, 2024

Ingredients

  • mushroom
  • butter
  • oinions
  • parsley
  • thyme
  • garlic
  • white wine (optional)

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in the pan
  2. Add oinions
  3. Add mushrooms and stir
  4. Add white wine to deglase (optional)
  5. Add thyme and parley
  6. Add the garlic
  7. Finish with pepper and salt
